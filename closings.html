Closings & Emergency Info

Today:

This page advises the University of Connecticut community regarding weather-related closings.

For non-weather-related emergencies at any campus other than the UConn Health Center, please go to the Alert Notification System website. For non-weather-related emergencies at the UConn Health Center, go to the Health Center's Alert Notification System website.

Campus contact information for weather-related closings:

  • Storrs: 24-hour emergency closing information line: 860-486-3768
  • Avery Point: 860-405-9000 or 860-405-9020
  • Greater Hartford: 860-570-9327
  • School of Law: 860-570-5145
  • Stamford: Go to the website or call 203-251-8524
  • Torrington: 860-626-6808
  • UConn Health Center: Go to the website
  • Waterbury: Go to the website or call 203-236-9800
For emergency closing policy and procedures, please see the Human Resources website.
